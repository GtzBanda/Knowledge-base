# Rock

![Rock Music](../images/rock.jpg)

## Overview

Rock music is one of the most influential genres in modern music. It developed during the 1950s by combining elements of blues, country, and rhythm and blues. Since then, rock has grown into a worldwide genre that has inspired countless musicians and shaped popular music for decades.

Electric guitars are one of the defining instruments of rock music. Many rock musicians also use effects such as overdrive, distortion, and delay to create powerful rhythm guitar parts, memorable solos, and unique sounds. The genre continues to evolve while remaining one of the most recognizable styles of music.

## What Makes Rock Unique

Some of the defining characteristics of rock music include:

- Strong guitar riffs
- Steady drum beats
- Memorable vocals
- Expressive guitar solos
- A wide variety of musical styles

## Popular Rock Styles

Rock music has evolved into many different styles, including:

- Classic rock
- Alternative rock
- Punk rock
- Hard rock
- Indie rock

> "Rock music continues to inspire generations of musicians around the world."
