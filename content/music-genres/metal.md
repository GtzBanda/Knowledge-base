# Metal

![Metal Music](../images/metal.jpg)

## Overview

Metal is a high-energy genre that grew out of hard rock during the late 1960s and early 1970s. It is known for heavy guitar riffs, powerful drumming, fast tempos, and high-gain guitar tones. Many metal guitarists rely on distortion pedals and powerful amplifiers to achieve their signature sound.

Over the years, metal has evolved into many different styles, each with its own sound and techniques. While some subgenres focus on speed and technical precision, others emphasize melody, atmosphere, or aggressive vocals. Despite these differences, the guitar remains one of the defining instruments of the genre.

## What Makes Metal Unique

Some of the defining characteristics of metal include:

- Heavy guitar riffs
- Fast tempos
- Powerful drumming
- High-gain guitar tones
- Technical guitar playing

## Popular Metal Subgenres

Metal has expanded into many subgenres, including:

- Thrash metal
- Death metal
- Progressive metal
- Metalcore
- Power metal

> "Metal pushes the limits of speed, precision, and power."
